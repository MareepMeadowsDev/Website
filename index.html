Frequently Asked Questions

What is this server about?

We're all about mareep!

How can I be a staff member?

We open staff applications as needed, there will be an announcement in our discord server when they are open.

How can I sponsor a giveaway

To sponsor a giveaway, you must be level 10+, please message anyone with Manage Mareep (giveaways) role for more info.

What do I get for boosting?

Boosters get tons of awesome stuff like our love and appreciation! Boosters get early access to ᠉・the-pasture, extra unboat currency, an auto reaction, and more!

How can I get the confessions consultant role?

Please message an admin or higher for the role, there are no requirements for the role but we do expect you to take it seriously.

How can I be a manager?

Please message the owner directly for more information.

How can I get the Artsy Mareep role?

Please message a staff member for more information.

How can I contact staff/make a report?

Please make a ticket in mareep-mail in order to contact staff. You can make a ticket for reports, to ask questions, to inquire about roles, and other things. Do not misuse it.

How can I make a suggestion for the server or website?

You can use the ~suggest [suggestion] command in either of the just-grazing channels to make a suggestion. You will receive a dm whenever your suggestion is approved, considered, or denied.

What can I do if I get banned/know someone banned?

Save the ban appeal form here, if your appeal is accepted, you will receive a message. Ban Appeals

How do I get access to the pasture?

There are multiple ways to get access, being a nitro booster and/or being level 30+ are among the "easiest" ways, although being an OG and/or having the @Trusted Mareep role also gives you access.

How can I post images/links

Please check the roles channel to see where and when you can post images and/or links.

What are the team roles for?

Team roles are a fun way to show your support for your favorite pokemon.

Why is there an Antarctica region role?

If you don't want people to know your region, you can get this role (or if for some reason you live in Antarctica).

How can I get the Trusted Mareep role?

This role is a special role for trustworthy, outstanding members of the server. Please do not ask for this role, if you deserve it, it will be given. It is up to the sole discretion of the Leader of Mareep.
